About Singapore

Singapore is a country, located in the Southeast of Asia. It shares a border with its neighbouring country, Malaysia.

Morden Singapore was founded in the 19th century by a man known as Sir Thomas Stamford Raffles. During that time the British empire was searching for a port in this region to base its merchants to compete with the Dutch to gain monoploy of the trade route. The British felt that Singapore was the most suitable place for a port due to its location and deep harbour. Hence, this was how morden Singapore was founded

It gained independence on 9 August 1965 and Lee Kuan Yew became its first president. As time went on, Singapore developed to a prosperous and wealthy city, known for its cleanliness and as a garden city.


Singapore is a country well known for its diverse culture. It's population is made up of Chinese, Malay, Indian, Caucasian and many various other ethnicities. One interesting aspect about it is how the multi-cultural element has shaped a slang spoken calles "Singlish", which consists of vocabulary and grammar being influenced by Malay, Hokkien, Cantonese, Teochew, Tamil and other languages found across Asia.
